Frequently Asked Questions

How do I activate my voucher?

Once you purchase a plan, your voucher balance is automatically added to your dashboard and starts tracking practice time instantly.

Does TranzyNote work during live meeting?

Yes. TranzyNote provides real-time AI support during calls, helping you respond confidently without switching tabs.

Can I upgrade my plan anytime?

Absolutely. You can upgrade from hourly bundles to unlimited plans at any time from your account settings.

What happens if my time runs out?

TranzyNote will pause your session when balance ends and notify you before it expires so you never lose progress.
